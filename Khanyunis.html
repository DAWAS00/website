Amina’s Panic

Amina, a 7-year-old girl from Gaza, had been struggling with the emotional scars left by the destruction of her neighborhood. Traumatized by the conflict, she suffered from panic attacks, nightmares, and a deep sense of fear. Her parents were desperate to find support for her mental health.

Amina’s Healing

After discovering our website, Amina’s parents were able to connect with professional
resources and support networks dedicated to helping children in crisis. Through the guidance
they received, they enrolled Amina in a counseling program offered by one of our partner organizations.

Over time, Amina began to heal. She slowly returned to her playful self,
found comfort in the safe spaces provided, and learned how to manage her anxiety.
Her story is a powerful reminder of the importance of accessible mental health
support and how a single resource can change a life.
